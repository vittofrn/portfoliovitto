About the project

Leave your usual space, enter the right one

A campaign created for the brand Scomodo, on the occasion of the opening of new locations, which serve as meeting places for students and young people.

Scomodo is a community founded in Rome in 2016 with the goal of driving social and cultural change through an active and engaged younger generation. With over 700,000 people reached, 500,000 copies distributed, and more than 4,000 events organized, Scomodo has become an authentic space for expression, dialogue, and collective growth.

The campaign I developed is built around the concept “Leave your usual space, enter the right one” — a direct invitation for young people to seek a free, inclusive place where they can express themselves, grow, and build meaningful relationships.

The visual identity relies on a dynamic, accessible illustration. This campaign works because it visually conveys Scomodo’s mission: to offer a real space where young people can grow, experiment, and feel part of a community.
