About the project

The devised game is an innovative Pet-Care and Collection Game centered on taking care of a virtual tree leaf.

With every bill received, the player obtains a new tree species to nurture, contributing to the growth of a personalized forest. Each tree becomes a symbol of the player’s environmental commitment.

The project combines gamification, sustainability, and behavioral strategies, with the goal of encouraging virtuous behaviors in an engaging and effective way.
